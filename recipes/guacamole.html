Guacamole

What is guacamole?

Guacamole is an avocado-based dip or spread that originated in Mexico. It's typically made with mashed avocado and lime juice, then seasoned with salt and cilantro. Guacamole often contains tomatoes and onions.


Ingredients:

  • 3 Avocados (duh)
  • Lime juice from 1 lime
  • A pinch of salt
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 tomatoes, diced
  • A handful of fresh cilantro
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • A pinch of cayenne pepper

The steps to make guacamole:

  1. Mash avocados together with the lime juice and salt
  2. Mix in onion, tomatoes, cilantro, garlic
  3. Stir in cayenne pepper
  4. Bingo, bango, bongo, you've got guacamole!
